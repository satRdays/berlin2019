Diversity Scholarships

As part of our commitment to making satRday Berlin as accessible as possible, we are offering a diversity scholarship program. The program aims at providing financial help to members of underrepresented or historically marginalized groups in the R community who may not otherwise be able to attend our conference for financial reasons.

Our scholarship consists of one full ticket to the event. Please note, that we cannot currently offer any funding for travel or accommodation.

Application

If you would like to apply for a scholarship, please fill out the form here by no later than Monday 27th May, 2019.

Applicants must:

  • belong to an underrepresented or historically marginalized group,
  • be over 18 years old at the date of application.

If you'd like some tips on applying, the Forwards Team have put together a guide here.

We will notify you about your application no later than Friday 31st May, 2019.
